Tournament Structure
HOW IT WORKS

🎓 This tournament is open exclusively to students currently studying in Luxembourg.

PHASE 01
🌐
Group Stage

Teams are split into 2 groups of 4 — Group A and Group B. Every team plays each other in their group in a Best of 1 match online. The top 2 teams from each group advance to the playoffs.

// ONLINE · BO1 · JUN 28 //
PHASE 02
⚔️
Playoff Semifinals

The top 2 from Group A face the top 2 from Group B in cross-group semifinals. Both matches are Best of 3 and streamed live. Winners go to the Grand Final.

// STREAMED · BO3 · JUN 29 //
PHASE 03
🏟️
Grand Final — LAN

The two semifinal winners meet in the Grand Final, played LAN in Luxembourg City. The first-ever Luxembourg CS Masters champion will be crowned here.

// LAN · LUXEMBOURG CITY · JUL 4 //

Who We Are
STUDENTS. GAMERS.
ORGANIZERS.

We are students at Athénée de Luxembourg, one of Luxembourg's most historic schools — and we're doing something a little different for our school project. The Luxembourg CS Masters was an idea born out of a genuine love for competitive gaming and a belief that Luxembourg deserves its own CS2 scene.

Counter-Strike has a way of bringing people together — the strategy, the clutch rounds, the late nights grinding with friends. We wanted to create an event that captures all of that, and give players in and around Luxembourg a real stage to compete on.

This is Season 1. We're building it from scratch, learning as we go, and putting our all into making this something people are genuinely proud to be part of — whether as a player, a viewer, or a supporter.

🎓 Eligibility: This tournament is open exclusively to students currently studying in Luxembourg. All participants must be enrolled in a school, college, or university in Luxembourg at the time of registration.

❤️ All proceeds from this tournament go directly to the Athénée de Luxembourg's NGO. By taking part — through registration, sponsorship, or simply watching — you're supporting a real cause backed by our school community. Gaming for good.

🎓
A School Project With Heart
Born inside Athénée de Luxembourg, this tournament is part coursework, part passion. We're applying skills in web design, event organisation, and marketing to something we genuinely care about — and the result is this.
🎮
Driven by a Love for Gaming
CS2 is more than a game to us. We play it, we watch it, we talk about it. This tournament is our way of giving back to a community that's given us so much, and putting Luxembourg on the esports map.
🌍
All Proceeds to Charity
Every euro raised goes to Athénée's NGO. This isn't a commercial venture — it's a community effort. We're proud to be doing something meaningful with something we love.
